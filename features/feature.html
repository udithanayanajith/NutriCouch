Child Health Development Record (CHDR) is a document which records growth and development details of a child from birth to adolescence. It is a tool which makes parents actively participate in and develop a greater understanding of their child's growth.

BP 100

BP-100™ is a Ready-to-Use therapeutic food (RUTF) in the form of a compressed biscuit/bar. It is used in the treatment of Severe Acute Malnutrition (SAM) in children and adults. BP-100™can be consumed as it is or crumbled into boiled drinking water and eaten as a porridge.

Symptoms of Nutrition Deficiencies
